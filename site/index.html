Drug repair sidecar

v11 ASR drug name repair — from 69% to 98.6% recall, zero false positives, no added infrastructure

v11 transcribes millions of calls accurately. Medical terminology breaks it.

Drug names are phonetically unpredictable — they fragment across word boundaries in ways standard ASR training data never covers. A formulary has thousands of active compounds; even a single specialist practice might use hundreds. v11's boost list caps at around 50 terms. Boosting your way out of this doesn't scale.

The sidecar fixes this without touching the model — and the same chassis works across legal, financial, and contact center domains.

Transparent proxy

Sits between v11 and your downstream consumer. No changes to the model, the client, or the integration contract.

Layered repair

Text-first phonetic path handles 95.7% of errors in under 35ms. Audio re-decode kicks in only for the hard cases.

Zero false positives

Context gate and suspect detection mean the pipeline only fires in medical context. Silence beats a wrong correction.

No new infrastructure

Re-decode reuses the v11 instance already in production. The sidecar's text path runs CPU-only.
